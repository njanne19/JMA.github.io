Hard Rock Cafe, Baby!

I can't believe it, we made it. This fall I was priveleged to perform at the Hard Rock Cafe of Boston. I was very nervous at first, because I had never performed in front of a crowd that large before, but they were a joy. It was a great first show outside of my usual home. Additionally, my good friend Nate Robards put together something special for you all to view; a nice little preview of the show. I hope you all enjoy, show it some love.
